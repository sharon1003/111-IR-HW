within epithelial macrophages usually clinically silent the condition rarely is associated with biliary symptoms or idiopathic pancreatitis and cannot reliably be detected by ultrasonography ultrasonography may reveal a thickened gallbladder wall with intramural diverticula adenomyomatosis may portend a higher risk of gallbladder malignancy most cases of cholesterolosis and
