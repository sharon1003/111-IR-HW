ask dr gourmet what is nicoise i know that recipes say nicoise but what does the term mean dr gourmet says nicoise is the french word for in the style of nice so any dish that is labeled nicoise would be in the cooking style of nice france usually these are recipes that have black olives tomatoes and anchovies the predominant flavoring is often garlic the most typical dish seen in america is salade nicoise this includes the olives tomatoes anchovies and a vinaigrette along with beans tuna and hard boiled eggs thanks for writingtimothy s harlan md facp dr gourmet
