decreasing fluid accumulation and by cooling and numbing the area apply ice for 20 minutes an hour every hour while awake for the first 24 hours do not apply ice directly to the injury to avoid potential cold injury to the site wrap the ice in a cloth or use a commercial ice pack a bag of frozen vegetables wrapped in a cloth makes a good ice pack protect the area from further injury avoid
