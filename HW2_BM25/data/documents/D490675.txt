the juvenile community service  restitution program vfy’s community service and restitution program works with orange county youth who have broken a law and are assigned community service hours young people are placed in nonprofit worksites throughout the county where volunteer supervisors oversee and evaluate their work program coordinators also organize group events such as park clean up projects and arts  crafts projects that benefit the community through this program youth learn important lessons in responsibility and accountability and often develop positive relationships with their adult supervisors young people are also taught valuable life and job skills while volunteering program coordinators scott dreyer – scottvolunteersforyouthorg nicole hunter – nicolevolunteersforyouthorg resources community service time sheet program requirements youth must sign up with their program coordinator to participate in any of the following projects community service opportunities please refer to your community service packet given to you at orientation or contact your community service coordinator for available community service opportunities
