usually provide parking for their guests only sometimes for an additional fee parking is available at the whalers village shopping center for a fee but they will validate your ticket in stores and restaurants if you go to one of the hotels for a luau there is usually parking for the event also for a fee in most cases there is a shuttle between main kaanapali hotels and whalers village center
