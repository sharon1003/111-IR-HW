caterpillarcom this box view · talk the caterpillar 943 crawler loader built by caterpillar inc was introduced in 1980 in the usa and france contents  showspecification edit the caterpillar 943 has a fourcylinder caterpillar 3204 engine direct injection producing 80 hp 60 k w fwhp it has a hydrostatic transmission it comes standard with a rollover protection system rops cab electronic monitoring system ems sealed and lubricated track salt oscillating undercarriage zbar linkage and a 142inch 36cm track shoe width the low ground pressure lgp units 19z and 04z are fitted with a 67inch 170cm track gauge the 943 was manufactured the following serial number prefixsin the usa with a 04z or 31yin france with a 03y or 19z prefix production of the 04z and 31y ended in the usa in 1985 production of 03y ended in france in 1987 production of 19z ended in france in 1992 updates edit in 1983 caterpillar increased the digging depth beginning with 03y01345 19z00236 04z00307 and 31y01334 a new scroll fuel system was added beginning with 03y01386 and 19z00253 in 1984 cat changed the loader frame and added a new brake control system and dual setting relief valve beginning with 04z00364 and 31y01339 at the same time the 31y unit received an attachment idler guard in 1985 the undercarriage was modified beginning with 03y01594 and 19z00341 in 1987 cat added a horizontal hydrostatic filler base beginning with 03y01806 and 19z00399 in 1989 the track roller frame was changed and the equalizer bar was shortened beginning with 19z01459 in 1990 cat added a generalpurpose bucket with fabricated hinges and a vertical face on the back beginning with 19z01580 serial number data edit based on data from the sales database of ritchie bros auctioneers it is estimated that more than 5900
