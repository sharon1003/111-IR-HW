supply malfunction or power surge this is especially useful in a device using ttl components as these are very sensitive to overvoltage however there are many other devices which can be damaged by overvoltage a crowbar circuit works by sensing a voltage that is above a certain threshold and shorting out the power supply this causes a voltage drop in the rest of the circuit and current surge through the power supply that will trip a circuitbreaker or blow a fuse the circuit must have a fuse or circuitbreaker as without it the power supply or the crowbar circuit will be damaged crowbar
