donatella and gianni learned about designing from their mother who had a dressmaking business i was the baby of the family she later explained to the new yorker i was so spoiled i was the bestdressed little girl in the city versace began developing her trademark look as a teenager — dyeing her hair platinum blonde and using dark eyeliner she was remarkably close to her older brother gianni who
