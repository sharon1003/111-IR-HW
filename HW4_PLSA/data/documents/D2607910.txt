cow island the most northern of the islands cow island is located just a few miles south of kaplan on la 35 the community and the drive there offer hidden treasures for locals and visitors who are on to them just before you get there on the corner of la 35 and la 335 youll find suires grocery stop in for a few things but dont leave without tasting their menu their culinary skills are well known in the area and youll believe it when you see people carrying plate lunches out by the bag full place an order and have a seat its worth the wait continuing on la 35 s stop at maxies five oaks they offer everything from fishing lures to hats and an atm machine well of course theres boudin too and possibly an alligator toy for the young ones driving through these areas youll be captivated by what seems to be an extensive and open landscape type there are no listings for this town
