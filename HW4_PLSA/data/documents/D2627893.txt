anthemis is a genus of aromatic flowering plants in the family asteraceae closely related to chamaemelum and like that genus known by the common name chamomile some species are also called dogfennel or mayweed anthemis are native to the mediterranean region and southwest asia east to iran a number of species have also become naturalized in the united kingdom and other parts of the world 3 4 5 6anthemis species are used as food plants by the larvae of some lepidoptera species including orthonama obstipata the gem and bucculatrix anthemidella a leafminer which feeds exclusively on anthemis
