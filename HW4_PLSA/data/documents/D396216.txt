examples of baroque quartets are telemanns paris quartets jazz  editquartets are popular in jazz and jazz fusion music jazz quartet ensembles are often composed of a horn classically clarinet or saxophone trumpet etc  a chordal instrument eg electric guitar piano hammond organ etc  a bass instrument eg double bass tuba or bass guitar and a drum kit this configuration is sometimes modified by
